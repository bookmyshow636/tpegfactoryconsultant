What is Industrial Setup Consulting?

TPEG International offers end-to-end consulting and execution for setting up industrial businesses in UAE. Whether you’re a small manufacturer or a large-scale producer, we handle every single step — so you can focus on growth, not paperwork.

Business & Setup Advisory

  • Industry-specific guidance for entering UAE.
  • Choosing between Freezone, Mainland, or Industrial Zones.
  • Legal structure and licensing strategy.

We guide you through legal structuring, licensing, and selecting the right UAE business zone to fit your industry goals.

Location Identification & Land Acquisition

  • Shortlisting and site visits of industrial plots.
  • Assistance in leasing or purchasing land.
  • Coordination with local authorities.

We help shortlist ideal industrial plots, arrange site visits, and manage all leasing or purchasing processes with local authorities.

Design, Development & Approvals

  • Architectural design and civil planning.
  • Factory layout as per production and compliance needs.
  • Approvals from relevant departments and municipalities.

Our team handles architectural design, factory layout planning, and ensures all construction and operational approvals are obtained seamlessly.

Machinery Procurement & Installation

  • Vendor selection, quotations, and procurement support.
  • Import logistics, customs clearance (duty-free).
  • Supervision of setup and commissioning.

From vendor selection to duty-free import and installation, we ensure smooth machinery procurement and efficient production setup.

Infrastructure & Utilities Setup

  • Electricity, water, sewage, and HVAC integration.
  • Fire safety, sustainability, and automation support.
  • IT infrastructure and connectivity.

We manage all utility setups including electricity, water, HVAC, safety systems, and IT infrastructure to support your factory operations.

Logistics & Supply Chain Support

  • Supply chain planning for inbound/outbound logistics
  • Warehousing and fleet setup.
  • Local and international logistics partners

We develop complete logistics plans, from warehousing to transport, using trusted partners for local and global supply chain needs.

Recruitment & HR Setup

  • Visa processing and labor onboarding.
  • Drafting labor contracts and HR policies.
  • Local recruitment partner coordination.

We support HR setup, visa processing, labor hiring, contracts, and connect you with local recruitment partners for workforce onboarding.

Compliance, Audits & Operational Launch

  • Factory audits and safety compliance.
  • Trial production and pre-launch checks.
  • Soft and full launch support.

We ensure regulatory compliance, perform audits, supervise trial production, and support both soft and full-scale factory launch.
